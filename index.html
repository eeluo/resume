 Research Experience

  •  Innovation and entrepreneurship training project for college students (National Level)

    Date: 2016.10-2018.9

    Research Topics: Research on the problem of pedestrian flow diversion in teaching buildings based on computer vision and indoor navigation technology

    Research Content: We apply motion target detection and tracking to the teaching building's pedestrian flow statistics, grasp the real-time number of people in each channel, and at the same time use indoor navigation and positioning technology to intelligently give clear and reasonable indoor navigation tips for the mobile people in the teaching building to realize the teaching building's pedestrian flow evacuation.

  •  National Natural Science Foundation of China Project

    Date:2019.9-2020.12

    Research Topics: Demonstration and Validation of Integrated Integration of Space-based Information Network for In-orbit Processing and Real-time Transmission

    Research Content: This project is a national major research program integration project that aims to develop a ground-based integrated demonstration and validation system for in-orbit processing and real-time transmission of space-based information networks, consisting of four modules with eleven subsystems. My research in this project is to develop and implement a satellite telemetry data management system.

  •  Popular Creativity Project of Dongfeng Motor Group Co.

    Date: 2021.3-2021.9

    Research Topics: Dongfeng taxi Order Demand Prediction

    Research Content: An algorithmic framework based on deep graph neural networks is applied to predict taxi demand, which aims to predict the future taxi order demand from historical taxi order data. I propose a taxi demand forecasting method based on graph convolutional networks and temporal convolutional networks, which effectively improves the accuracy of taxi demand forecasting.
